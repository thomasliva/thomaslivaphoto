River of light

The Aurora Borealis, a gift of light and colour from our sun. Created from electrically charged particles flinged out from the sun due to solar storms. Crashing into our magnetic field and displaying colours of green and purple. From the softest streak of green, to a full coverage of the whole sky.

Let your eyes and mind sink into the deep colours and patterns created naturally over the sky.

Wake up

That early morning glow, just before sunrise. When the world is still sleeping, and everything is quiet. A moment to reflect, before everything wakes up.
