Purchase and pricing

    There are two ways to purchase my photography depending on where you live.

    Option 1 international and domestic:

    You can either push here or the prints button in the header. This will take you directly to Fine Art America. Here you can browse and choose, and then select the medium and size you want. All will be sent directly to your address. Note! These are not signed and numbered.

    Option 2 domestic:

    The other option is to send me a mail here or a dm through facebook or instagram. Here we can have a chat regarding different medium and recommended sizes, and from there I will fix everything for you locally. Note! This option is only available for Norwegian residents.

    But you can always send me a dm to talk if you have some questions either way.

    All photos comes in an edition of 30 pieces. Domestic sold photographs will be signed and numbered.
